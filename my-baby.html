About My Babies

Introduction

I have two babies:

  • one is very old - born in 1995
  • one is very young - barely half a year

They are both boys.

The big one

The one from 1995 is unique, he is the love of my life.

  • He lifts me up when I am down.
  • He picks me up when I have fallen.
  • Cheers me up when I am sad.
  • Knows how to throw down a great meal
  • and most importantly, gives me a good laugh

Sometimes, he can be very difficult to understand or be with. I still pray to God that he is the one for me, since I enjoy his company more than anyone else.

The not so tiny but tiny one

This one came to me as a surprise. Actually when I had accumulated enough money to by a sportsbike. Little did I know that money would be used for diapers

I must admit this baby is a handful, but I cannot even imagine a life without him

I love my babies
